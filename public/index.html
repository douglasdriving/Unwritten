Welcome to Unwritten!

Unwritten is a collaborative writing game in which a community of players writes choose-your-own-adventures together. Join the community and take part in the creation of an ever-expanding world!

To play the current version of Unwritten, you can create an account here. To play, read the scenarios and choose what action you wanna perform to move forward through the story. If none of the existing actions appeals to you, you can always add a new one to branch the story in a new direction. Keep playing until you reach an endpoint, and continue adding you own ideas to from there.
