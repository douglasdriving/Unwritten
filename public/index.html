Unwritten

Welcome to Unwritten - a game where the story lies in the hands of the players. Here, you are both a player and a creator. Experience a collaboratively written branching narrative and contribute with your own ideas to expand the world.

To play, read the scenarios and choose what action you wanna perform to move forward through the story. If none of the existing actions appeals to you, you can always add a new one to branch the story in a new direction. Keep playing until you reach an endpoint, and continue adding you own ideas to from there.
