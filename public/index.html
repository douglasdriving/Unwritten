Welcome to Unwritten!

Unwritten is a collaborative writing game in which a community of players writes choose-your-own-adventures together. As a player, you get to not only read through stories written by others, but also add your own ideas to the world. With time, the world of Unwritten will expand to include hundreds of branches and storylines to explore, all created by the players. Join the community and take part in the creation of an ever-expanding world!

How to Play

If you want to try the current version of Unwritten, you can create an account here. To play, read the scenarios and choose what action you wanna perform to move forward through the story. If none of the existing actions appeals to you, you can always add a new one to branch the story in a new direction. Keep playing until you reach an endpoint, and continue adding you own ideas to from there.

Development

Unwritten is currently in an early prototyping stage where weekly updates are sent out to a group of playtesters. Feedback from these playtesters is used continuously to improve the game. The current goal is to keep expanding and refining the design of Unwritten until it has reached a point where it is ready to be packaged and released as version 1.0.
